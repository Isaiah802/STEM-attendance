Our Story

Founded in 2020, STEM Club began with a small group of passionate students who wanted to create a community where innovation and learning in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics could thrive. What started as informal meet-ups has grown into a vibrant organization with over 150 active members.

Our club has evolved from simple coding sessions to hosting major competitions, workshops with industry professionals, and collaborative projects that have real-world impact. We believe that every student has the potential to make a difference through STEM, and we're here to provide the resources, support, and community to make that happen.
