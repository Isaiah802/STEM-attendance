Got questions? We've got answers! Find everything you need to know about joining, participating, and making the most of STEM Club.

Who can join STEM Club? ‚ñº

STEM Club is open to all students regardless of major, year, or experience level. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, there's a place for you in our community!

How do I join? ‚ñº

Simply attend one of our meetings and sign in using the attendance form. You can also fill out our contact form to get added to our mailing list for updates on meetings and events.

When and where do you meet? ‚ñº

Meeting times and locations vary by event and project. Check our Events page for the latest schedule including workshops, project work sessions, and general meetings.

Are there membership fees? ‚ñº

No! STEM Club is completely free to join and participate in. All our workshops, projects, and events are open to members at no cost.

What kind of events do you host? ‚ñº

We host hands-on workshops covering programming, electronics, and robotics. Our flagship project this semester is the Solar UAV. We also organize social events to build community. Check the Events page for upcoming activities!

Do I need programming experience? ‚ñº

Not at all! We welcome students of all skill levels. We offer beginner-friendly workshops and have mentorship programs to help new members get started. Many of our most active members started with zero experience.

Can I propose my own project or workshop idea? ‚ñº

Absolutely! We encourage members to bring their ideas. Reach out through our Contact page or speak with any officer at a meeting. Member-driven initiatives help shape the club!

How can I become more involved? ‚ñº

Join one of the four Solar UAV sub-teams (Mechanical, Electronics, Software, or Documentation), volunteer to lead a workshop, help plan events, or run for an officer position at elections. Contact us to learn about current opportunities!

Do you offer help with coursework or studying? ‚ñº

While we don't provide formal tutoring, many members form study groups and help each other. The skills you learn in workshops and projects often complement coursework. Check our Resources page for learning materials!

How do I stay updated on club activities? ‚ñº

Fill out our Contact form to get added to our mailing list. You can also check the Events page regularly for the latest schedule and announcements.

We're here to help! Reach out to us anytime.
