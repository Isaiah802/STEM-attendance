No! STEM Club is completely free to join. We're funded through the university and occasionally through sponsorships, so all our events, workshops, and resources are available at no cost.

We host a variety of events including coding workshops, robotics competitions, guest speakers from industry, hackathons, study groups, project showcases, and social events. There's something for everyone!

Absolutely! We encourage members to bring their ideas. You can submit project proposals through our Projects page or speak with any officer during a meeting. We love member-driven initiatives!

While we don't provide formal tutoring, many members form study groups and help each other with coursework. We also maintain a resource library and organize peer learning sessions for common STEM courses.
